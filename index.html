About Me

I’ve been working in a Computer Repair Shop during my pcto stage.

My name is Filippo, and during this school year I completed a two-week stage at Umbria Computers. During this experience, I worked on diagnosing and repairing computers, installing operating systems, replacing hardware components, and solving technical issues.
